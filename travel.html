Travel & Lodging

Our venue is located in Medina, Ohio — about 35 minutes from Cleveland Hopkins International Airport. There’s plenty of free parking available on site for all guests.

While we aren’t providing hotel accommodations, if you’re coming from out of town, we recommend checking hotels or Airbnb options in the Medina or Brunswick areas.
